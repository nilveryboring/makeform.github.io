# Form Analytics

Use form analytics to understand submissions, answer patterns, and how people move through your form.

<Callout type="info">
Makeform gives you response summaries inside the form dashboard. You can also connect Google Analytics 4 for traffic analytics on published forms.
</Callout>

## What you can review

Open a form and go to its summary or submissions area to review:

- Completed submissions
- Partial submissions when enabled
- Recent responses
- Answer summaries for choice, rating, ranking, and scale questions
- Individual submission details
- Exportable response data

The summary view is best for patterns. The submissions view is best for individual records.

## Review response summaries

Use summaries when you want to answer questions like:

- Which option was selected most often?
- What is the average rating?
- Which ranking options performed best?
- Are respondents skipping a field?
- What are people writing in long text answers?

For qualitative forms, summaries help you spot themes before exporting data.

## Review individual submissions

Use the submissions view when you need the exact response from one person.

This is useful for:

- Lead review
- Support intake
- Applications
- Orders
- Payment-related responses
- Forms with uploaded files or signatures

## Connect Google Analytics 4

Use GA4 when you need traffic analytics outside Makeform, such as page views, acquisition source, campaigns, or behavior across a larger website funnel.

1. Open your form.
2. Go to the <span className="text-cyan-500 font-semibold bg-cyan-100 px-2 py-1 rounded">Settings</span> tab.
3. Find <span className="text-cyan-500 font-semibold bg-cyan-100 px-2 py-1 rounded">Google Analytics</span>.
4. Turn it on.
5. Enter a GA4 Measurement ID, such as `G-XXXXXXXXXX`.
6. Save or publish the form.

<Callout type="warning">
GA4 uses a Measurement ID, not a Universal Analytics tracking ID. The expected format starts with `G-`.
</Callout>

## When to use Makeform analytics vs GA4

Use Makeform analytics for response quality and submission data.

Use GA4 for traffic source, visitor behavior, and website-level reporting.

Most teams use both: Makeform for form answers, GA4 for acquisition and funnel context.

## Testing checklist

- Submit a test response and confirm it appears in submissions.
- Check the summary view after multiple responses.
- If using GA4, verify the Measurement ID starts with `G-`.
- Open the published form and confirm events appear in your GA4 realtime view.
- Keep privacy and consent requirements in mind when using third-party analytics.

## Troubleshooting

### No analytics appear yet

Submit at least one test response. Summary charts need response data.

### GA4 does not receive traffic

Check the Measurement ID and make sure you are testing the published form URL.

### Summary numbers look different from GA4

That can happen. Makeform summaries are based on submissions and answers. GA4 is based on web analytics events and visitor sessions.
