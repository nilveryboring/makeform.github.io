# Customize your link settings and meta information

You can customize the link text and meta information of your form.

<div className="shadow-2xl mt-12">
  ![Sharing Setting Example1](/images/share-setting1.png)
</div>

In the <span className="text-cyan-500 font-bold bg-cyan-200 px-2 py-1 rounded">Share</span> tab,
clicking the <span className="text-slate-500 font-bold bg-slate-200 px-2 py-1 rounded">Customize text</span> button
and the <span className="text-slate-500 font-bold bg-slate-100 px-2 py-1 rounded">Customize image</span> button
to customize the link text and meta information of your form.

Then you can either copy the link, or download the QR code to share with others.
