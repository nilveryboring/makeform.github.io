# Introduction

Makeform is an AI-powered form builder that revolutionizes the way you create and manage forms. Built as a modern alternative to traditional form builders, it combines the simplicity of online form builders with advanced AI capabilities and professional features.

## What is Makeform?

Makeform is a next-generation form builder that enables anyone to create intelligent, conversational forms, surveys, and quizzes without any coding knowledge. By leveraging AI technology, Makeform helps you:

- **Design Beautiful Forms**: Create visually appealing forms with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface
- **Generate AI Content**: Let AI help you write questions, responses, and form content
- **Analyze Responses**: Get intelligent insights from your form submissions with AI-powered analytics
- **Automate Workflows**: Connect your forms with popular tools and automate your workflow
