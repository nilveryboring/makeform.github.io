## Customize Your Own Form

- **Edit Button**: Open the Edit Field menu to modify the field's properties or Delete the element.
  ![Customize Button](/images/customize_open.png)

---

- **Rearrange Elements**: Rearrange the position of elements by dragging up and down.
  ![Customize Rearrange](/images/customize_rearrange.png)

---

- **Edit Fields**: Modify existing fields or add new ones by interacting with the AI assistant.
- **Design Customization**: Adjust the form's appearance to align with your brand by selecting themes, colors, and fonts.

![Customize Button](/images/customize_button.png)
