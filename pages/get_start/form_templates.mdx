## Form Templates

- You can choose a template for your form from a bunch of templates by clicking on the `Templates` button.
  ![Template Button](/images/template_button.png)

---

**Templates**:

- **Categories**: The Categories panel that helps users filter templates by type, such as Feedback Forms, Signup Forms, and Survey Forms.
- **Templates Gallery**: The Templates Gallery showing previews of different form templates with names, categories, and download counts.
  ![Template Examples](/images/template_examples.png)
