## General

### Anti-Bot Protection (Level 1)

Enable honeypot field detection to automatically block spam submissions. Recommended for all public forms to reduce spam.

### Reduce Motion

Disable page transition effects for simpler and faster navigation. Improves accessibility for users who prefer reduced motion.

### Redirect on Completion

Redirect respondents to a custom URL after form submission. URL must start with `http://` or `https://`.

### Hidden Fields

Add custom hidden fields to capture additional data like UTM parameters, user IDs, or referral codes without showing them to respondents. Field names must start with a letter and can only contain letters, numbers, and underscores.

## Branding

<Callout type="info">
Branding customization requires a [Pro plan](../plans-and-pricing).
</Callout>

### Show Loading Screen

Display a loading screen when your form first loads.

### Show Custom Message on Loading Screen

Display a custom message on the loading screen instead of the default text. Maximum 100 characters.

### Show "Powered by" Badge

Show or hide the "Powered by Makeform" badge on your form. Hidden by default for Pro users.

### Show Makeform Link on End Page

Show or hide the Makeform link on the thank you page after form submission.

---

## Tracking

### Google Analytics (GA4)

Track form views and interactions with Google Analytics 4. Enter your GA4 Measurement ID (format: `G-XXXXXXXXXX`). [Learn more](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9304153) about finding your Measurement ID.

<Callout type="info">
Requires a [Pro plan](../plans-and-pricing).
</Callout>

---

## Access

### Close Form

Prevent new responses to your form. When enabled, visitors will see a custom message instead of the form.

### Show Custom Message on Closed Form

Customize the message shown when your form is closed. Include a title and description to inform visitors why the form is unavailable.

---

## Behavior

### Save Answers for Later

Allow respondents to save their progress in the browser and return later. Progress is saved locally on the same device and browser. Enabled by default.

### Auto-Jump to Next Page

Automatically advance to the next page when a question is answered. Works with single multiple choice (without "Other" option), single select, rating, or opinion scale questions. Only one question per page.
