## How to Create Your First Form

### 1. Sign Up and Access the Dashboard

- **Sign Up**: Click on the <span className="text-cyan-500 font-bold bg-cyan-100 px-2 py-1 rounded">Try for Free</span> button on the homepage to enter the Dashboard directly.
- **Access Dashboard**: Once logged in, you'll be directed to your personal dashboard where you can manage all your forms.

![Try For Free](/images/try_for_free.png)

---

### 2. Create a New Form

- **Start a Conversation**: Click on the <span className="text-cyan-500 font-bold bg-cyan-100 px-2 py-1 rounded">New Form</span> button to initiate a chat with our AI assistant.
- **Define Form Purpose**: In the chat interface, describe the type of form you need to the AI chatbot (e.g., "I need a customer feedback survey").
- **AI Generation**: The AI will instantly generate a form based on your description, including appropriate fields and questions.
