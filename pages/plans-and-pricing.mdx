# Plans & Pricing

Build beautiful forms for free, unlock advanced features with Pro and Business.

---

## Free Plan

Makeform is free to use with no limits on forms, submissions, or most features. Create unlimited forms and collect responses without any cost.

**What's included in Free:**
- Unlimited forms and submissions
- All input types (30+ field types)
- Multi-page forms
- File uploads (10 MB per file)
- Email notifications
- Conditional logic
- Form analytics
- Slack integration
- Embedded forms
- Basic AI assistance
- Standard support

<Callout type="info">
The Free plan is perfect for individuals, side projects, and small teams getting started with form building.
</Callout>

---
